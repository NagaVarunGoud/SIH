Coal Mining Laws & Regulations

Mines and Minerals (Development and Regulation) Act, 1957 (MMDR Act): Governs the regulation of mines and the development of minerals, including coal.

Coal Mines (Special Provisions) Act, 2015: Provides for the allocation of coal mines through auctions to ensure transparency and fair practices.

Environmental Impact Assessment (EIA) and other regulations ensure minimal impact on the environment.
